# Account Setup

## Creating Your First Account

### Registration Process

1. **Go to the registration page**
   Navigate to the BlokMap registration page via your institution's website.

2. **Fill in your details**
   - Full name
   - Email address (use your student email or work email)
   - Choose a strong password
   - Select your role (Student, Authority, or Institution)

3. **Confirm your email**
   Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the link to activate your account.

### Account Types

#### Student Account
- Access to location search
- Make and manage reservations
- Profile management

#### Authority Account
- All student features
- Manage members within organization
- Add and manage locations
- View member reservations

#### Institution Account
- System-wide configuration
- User management
- Reports and analytics
- Platform settings

## First Login

### Logging In
1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"

### Dashboard Overview
After logging in, you'll see the dashboard with:
- Quick actions based on your role
- Recent activity
- Important notifications
- Navigation to main features

## Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password:
1. Click "Forgot password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to set a new password

## Login Problems

### Common Issues
- **Wrong email**: Make sure you're using the correct email address
- **Forgotten password**: Use the password reset function
- **Account not activated**: Check your inbox for the activation email
- **Blocked account**: Contact the system administrator

### Getting Help
If you can't figure it out, contact:
- Your local IT support
- Your institution's system administrator
- The BlokMap support team via GitHub
