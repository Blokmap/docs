# User Manual

Welcome to the BlokMap user manual! This guide helps you use the BlokMap platform, regardless of whether you're a student, authority, or institution.

## What is BlokMap?

BlokMap is a platform that makes it easy to find locations and make reservations within educational institutions. The system is designed for three main user groups:

- **Students**: Search for available locations and make reservations
- **Authorities**: Manage members and locations within their organization
- **Institutions**: System administration and platform configuration

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
If you don't have an account yet, you can easily create one via the registration page. You'll get access to features based on your role within the institution.

### First Login
After creating your account, you can log in with your credentials. Depending on your role, you'll see different options in the dashboard.
